The rules

  • Pawn .- Can only move two steps at beginning, can take down another piece diagonally. It can be promoted to another piece besides the king
  • Rook.- Can only move in straight lines. Cannot jump over pieces.
  • Knight.- Moves in an “L” shape: two squares in one direction (horizontal or vertical), then one square perpendicular to that. Can jump over pieces. Captures on the square it lands.
  • Bishop.- Moves any number of squares diagonally. Cannot jump over pieces. Each bishop stays on its starting color (one on light squares, one on dark squares).
  • Queen.- Combines the powers of the rook and bishop. Cannot jump over pieces.
  • King.- Moves one square in any direction (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal).
